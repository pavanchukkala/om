About Us

OM Alloy Castings, established in 2017 at Industrial Park Naidupeta, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh, specializes in manufacturing premium-quality excavator bucket teeth and metal components. Promoted by technocrats with technical expertise, our company focuses on delivering innovative solutions in the casting industry.

We use advanced Lost Foam Casting (LFC) technology to overcome challenges in surface finish, quality, and mass production while meeting the demands of local and global markets.

Our Mission

  • Manufacturing defect-free castings using the latest technologies.
  • Ensuring consistent chemical composition and suitable heat treatment processes.
  • Focusing on customer satisfaction by solving problems effectively.
  • Improving working conditions and infrastructure to meet ISO standards.

Vision

To be a leading manufacturer of high-quality, wear-resistant casting parts, trusted by customers for durability and performance.
